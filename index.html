Methodology & Data Provenance

Read before citing — mix of measured and estimated fields

Measured: per-game lifetime plays/visits scraped from the astrocade.com gallery cards (15 featured titles). Platform KPIs (MAU, monthly plays, game count, funding, Discord size) sourced from Astrocade, Fortune, and GlobeNewswire.

Estimated / assigned: Genre is analyst-classified from each game's title and description — Astrocade exposes no public genre field.

Not available: per-game likes and shares are rendered client-side by the site's SPA and are not present in fetchable HTML. They are intentionally omitted rather than fabricated. To capture them, a logged-in browser-automation pass over each game page would be required.
